BellyBallot.com

BellyBallot is a site for pregnant parents choosing baby names. The site decided on NoSQL over a relational db in order to serve name information to visitors who require fast page loads and wide variability in data querying. The community of users on the site will also be contributing to the meaning of the names and other data, so the data structure needed massive scalability as the name db grows.
